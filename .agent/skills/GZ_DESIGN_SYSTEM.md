# GZ Luxury Design System
> **Inheritance**: `ui-ux-pro-max.md` ruleset active.

## Signature Characteristics
- **Tone**: High-End Editorial / Luxury Hospitality. The aesthetic must feel like a premium print magazine layout natively translated to the web.
- **Palette**: 
  - Gold (`#C5A059`) for primary accents.
  - Deep Black (`#1A1A1A`) for grounding elements.
  - Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) for negative space to ensure content breathes.

## Typography
- **Headers (Luxury/Traditional)**: Serif fonts (e.g., *Playfair Display* or *Bodoni*). Used to assert history, trust, and luxury.
- **Body (Modern/Clean)**: Sans-Serif fonts (e.g., *Inter* or *Geist*). Used for dense copy to guarantee accessibility and high contrast readability.

## Luxury Patterns
- **Glassmorphism**: Utilize frosted glass effects (`backdrop-blur-md bg-black/40`) for headers, sticky elements, and dialogs.
- **Liquid Glass Overlay**: Ensure hero structures have a dark liquid gradient mask to enhance text visibility.
- **Staggered Layouts**: Break symmetry. Alternate image shapes and text blocks to create organic, non-linear reading flows.
